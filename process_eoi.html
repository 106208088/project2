Overview

SWC IT values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle personal data collected through our recruitment website and application form.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • Job application details including qualifications, employment history, and skills.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and session information (for analytics).

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for recruitment purposes, including:

  1. Assessing suitability for employment.
  2. Contacting you about your application.
  3. Statistical reporting to improve our recruitment process.

Data Storage and Security

We store applicant information securely on servers within Australia. Data is protected using encryption and access controls. Only authorized HR staff can access personal data.

Disclosure

We will not share your personal information with third parties except where required by law or where you have provided consent.

Access and Correction

You may request access to your personal data or correction of any inaccuracies by contacting us at privacy@swcit.com.au.

Use of Cookies

This site may use cookies to improve functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting access to the main content.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@swcit.com.au.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
